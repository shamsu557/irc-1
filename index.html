About Ibadurrahman College

Ibadurrahman College is a premier institution dedicated to nurturing well-rounded individuals through a unique blend of Islamic and Formal Education.

Our Islamic Education program, Halqatu Ibadurrahman, focuses on Quranic memorization and Islamic studies, fostering spiritual growth. Our Formal Education program equips students with academic skills for a globalized world.
